# ⚗️ EQSMP — Epic Questing Survival Multiplayer

**Minecraft Forge 1.20.1** · ~146 mods · co-op friendly · **fully bilingual quests (EN / PT-BR)**

EQSMP is a questing & survival pack built around a single idea: **no mod stands alone.** Recipes and progression deliberately pull content from **different mods into the same chain**, so the only way forward is to weave magic, machinery and exploration together — and reforge the legend that broke the world.

It shines in multiplayer, where players naturally specialize: one becomes the **smelter**, another the **mage**, another the **explorer** — and the server wins when they share the work.

## 🧩 The hook: cross-mod synergy

Most "kitchen-sink" packs are a pile of mods that ignore each other. EQSMP **chains them**. A simple copper ingot might travel through *Geolosys → custom ore tiers → Create → Createmetallurgy → Occultism or Integrated Dynamics*. Magic needs the tech's metals; tech needs the magic's reagents; exploration unlocks both. **No single mod closes the loop.**

## 🏛️ The four pillars

- 🔮 **Magic** — Ars Nouveau, Botania, Occultism, Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks (unified mana). Rituals, familiars, spell schools, magical automation.
- ⚙️ **Technology** — Create + Createmetallurgy, Integrated Dynamics, Applied Energistics 2. Mechanical engineering, smelting, logic, digital storage.
- 🗺️ **Exploration** — Geolosys realistic ore deposits, extended biomes, dungeons, and adventure bosses from **L_Ender's Cataclysm**, **Bosses of Mass Destruction** and **Twilight Forest**.
- 🤝 **Shared adventure** — a hand-built **FTB Quests** book (23 chapters, 500+ quests) guiding co-op progression.

## 🪨 The signature path: F1 → F4 → Philosopher's Stone

The backbone of the pack — four gate-items in sequence, **each requiring ingredients processed in different mods**, plus an endgame ritual that consumes all four:

1. **F1 — Magical Agglutinate** · Create compaction → Botania mana infusion
2. **F2 — Kinetic Core** · Create mechanical crafter → Occultism foliot ritual
3. **F3 — Pure Mana Crystal** · Botania runic altar → Ars Nouveau imbuement
4. **F4 — Spiritual Core** · Create sequenced assembly that consumes F1 + F2 + F3
5. **⭐ Philosopher's Stone** · a djinni ritual that consumes all four cores + a Nether Star

## ✨ Custom systems (KubeJS)

- **Ore Processing** — 10 quality tiers rolled at mining time; better pickaxe = more material; multiple processing routes; fully EMI/JEI-compatible drops.
- **Sin Difficulty** — killing peaceful/neutral mobs raises Scaling Health difficulty; killing hostiles lowers it (momentum-based).
- **Ore Sample Markers** — break a Geolosys surface sample and a map landmark appears (Surveyor / Antique Atlas) with the ore's icon and name.
- **Cross-mod gates**, tool rebalance, Botania flower decay, Ars archwood drops, and more.

## 🎁 Quality of life & extras

- **Bilingual quest book** — every quest title, subtitle and description in **English and Portuguese**; the game picks your language automatically.
- **Inventory Pets** as randomized quest rewards (tiered loot table).
- **Waystones**, **Tombstone** (death recovery), full storage suite (Sophisticated Storage/Backpacks, Storage Drawers), **Pam's HarvestCraft** cooking.
- **Simple Voice Chat**, **Oculus** shader support with a bundled shaderpack.
- Performance-tuned: Embeddium, Canary, C2ME (Forge), FerriteCore, Krypton Reforged, Starlight, ModernFix and more.

## 🧑‍💻 About this pack

EQSMP is made by **one single person** — there's no team. The bulk of the content was built **with the help of AI**: the scripts, recipes, progression, balancing, the **artwork and the custom items**, and even most of the documentation. It's a personal, experimental project that keeps evolving.

A maioria dos packs "kitchen-sink" é uma pilha de mods que se ignoram. O EQSMP **encadeia eles**. Um simples ingot de cobre pode passar por *Geolosys → tiers customizados de minério → Create → Createmetallurgy → Occultism ou Integrated Dynamics*. A magia precisa dos metais do tech; o tech precisa dos reagentes da magia; a exploração libera os dois. **Nenhum mod isolado fecha o ciclo.**
